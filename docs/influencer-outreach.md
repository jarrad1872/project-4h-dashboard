## 1. Executive Summary

### Why Influencer > Paid Ads for Trade Conversion

Contractors don't buy from ads. They buy from other contractors.

The single biggest trust signal in the trades is **peer endorsement**. A roofer trusts another roofer's recommendation more than any Facebook retargeting ad. YouTube has become the watercooler for trade professionals — it's where they learn, commiserate, and make buying decisions.

**Here's the math on why influencer beats paid:**
- Average CPL for home service/contractor software via paid search: **$80–$180/lead**
- Conversion rate from cold paid traffic: **2–5%**
- Influencer referral conversion rate (warm, peer-endorsed): **8–15%** (industry benchmark)
- Influencer trust carries: a mention at 200K subs with 10% trade audience = **20,000 warm prospects**

**The Saw.City deal is uniquely influencer-friendly:**
- 20% recurring commission (~$8/month at the $39 plan) for up to 24 months = **up to ~$192/contractor**
- Recurring passive income — not one-time affiliate
- Low price point ($39/mo) means easy yes from contractors with phone anxiety
- Co-branded landing page makes creators look like partners, not promoters

**This campaign targets the "1,000 true contractors" model** — each creator only needs to convert 10–50 contractors to generate ~$1,920–$9,600/year in recurring revenue. That's compelling for creators who are also business owners.

## 4. Deal Structure One-Pager

### What the Creator Gets

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Recurring Commission** | 20% recurring per referred contractor (~$8/month at current $39 pricing) |
| **Maximum Per Referral** | ~$192 (24 months × ~$8) |
| **No referral cap** | Unlimited referrals — unlimited income |
| **Unique promo code** | Creator-specific code (e.g. BRIANSLAWN) |
| **Co-branded landing page** | mow.city/brianslawn — looks like their product |
| **Free trial** | 30 days free for creator + their audience |
| **Monthly payout** | Direct deposit or PayPal, net 30 |

### How Tracking Works

1. **Unique promo code** — Creator's code entered at signup (e.g. BRIANSLAWN or ROGERWAKEFIELD)
2. **UTM tracking link** — Custom URL in description (e.g. saw.city/?ref=brianslawn) 
3. **Co-branded page** — mow.city/brianslawn has embedded UTM auto-populated
4. **Dashboard access** — Creator gets a live dashboard showing click count, signups, active subscribers, monthly earnings

Payout triggers at $50 minimum. Auto-paid monthly. If a subscriber cancels and resubscribes with same code, creator gets credit on the new subscription from month 1.

### What Saw.City Provides

- **Onboarding call** for the creator so they can genuinely demo the product
- **30-day free trial code** to offer their audience (e.g. "Try free for 30 days with code BRIANSLAWN")
- **Co-branded landing page** hosted on the trade-specific subdomain (mow.city, pipe.city, etc.)
- **Dedicated partner rep** — one human contact for the creator, not a ticketing system
- **Custom creative assets** — thumbnail graphics, video cutaway clips if needed

### Terms

- Payout continues for 24 months per subscriber from the date they signed up
- If creator's code generates a subscriber, that subscriber is "locked" to that creator for 24 months regardless of whether creator continues promoting
- Minimum payout: $50/month before transfer
- Creator can promote as many or as few times as they choose — no exclusivity required
